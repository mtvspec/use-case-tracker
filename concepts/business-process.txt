--------------------------------------------------------------------------------
-- Business Process (BP)
--------------------------------------------------------------------------------
a) A collection of related, structured activities or tasks that produce a
specific service or product (serve a particular goal) for a particular
customer.
--------------------------------------------------------------------------------
-- Input (I)
--------------------------------------------------------------------------------
a) A set of elements used for transformation by process to output.
Set elements may be:
a) Resource
b) Data
--------------------------------------------------------------------------------
-- Input supplier (IS)
--------------------------------------------------------------------------------
a) A an individual or process.
--------------------------------------------------------------------------------
-- Output (O)
--------------------------------------------------------------------------------
a) A set of elements received for output consumer.
Set of elements may be:
a) Product
b) Service
c) Data
--------------------------------------------------------------------------------
-- Output Consumer (OC)
--------------------------------------------------------------------------------
a) A an individual or process.
