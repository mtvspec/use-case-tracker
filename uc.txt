User (U)
--------------------------------------------------------------------------------
A stakeholder who interacts with the system to achieve its goals.
--------------------------------------------------------------------------------
Stakeholder
--------------------------------------------------------------------------------
A person, group or organization who affects or is affected by the software
system.
--------------------------------------------------------------------------------
Customer
--------------------------------------------------------------------------------
A stakeholder who is paying for the development of the system or who is
expected to purchase the system once it is complete.
--------------------------------------------------------------------------------
Requirement (RQ)
--------------------------------------------------------------------------------
What the software must do to satisfy the stakeholders.
